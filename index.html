Key Priorities for Gosforth

The issues that matter most to our community

🏣

Safer, Cleaner Streets

Pushing for better street lighting, regular maintenance, and tackling anti-social behaviour to make every part of Gosforth a safe place to live and walk.

🚌

Better Transport Links

Campaigning for improved bus services, safer cycling routes, and responsible traffic management that works for residents and local businesses alike.

🌱

Protecting Green Spaces

Defending Gosforth's parks and green corridors from overdevelopment, and investing in community gardens and play areas for families.

🏘

Affordable Housing

Supporting genuinely affordable new homes and pushing back against developments that don't meet the needs of local people and families.

🏫

Schools & Young People

Ensuring local schools get the funding and support they need, and expanding opportunities for children and young people across the ward.

🤝

Stronger Community

Supporting local businesses on Gosforth High Street, community groups, and making sure residents have a genuine voice in council decisions.
